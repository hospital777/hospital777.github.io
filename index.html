Services

emergency unit

make a difference in your lives and those you love, we offer you the emergency unit at andalusian hospital, which operates 24/7 with the same high levels of accuracy, and is ready to provide care that lives up to your confidence. the department features our qualified doctors from the european recovery council and the american heart association. the section also provides all devices from screens, ventilators, electric shocks and all the tools needed for all advanced recovery procedures. the emergency team is qualified to deal with all heart injuries, pulmonary injuries, wounds, infectious diseases, rta injuries, trauma, poisonings, burns, gastrointestinal diseases, some kidney diseases and neurological diseases.

Departments

Cardiology and intensive care

Cardiology is the study and treatment of disorders of the heart and the blood vessels. A person with heart disease or cardiovascular disease may be referred to a cardiologist. and A cardiac intensive care unit (CICU) provides the highest level of care for critically ill patients with serious heart conditions.

Pediatric

The specialty of pediatrics is one of the modern medical branches concerned with health care for children of all ages. Therefore, pediatrics may specialize in studying the psychological and physical development of the child, and may include treating various diseases and disabilities of children, and providing all means of medical and health care, whether they are newborns or in childhood

Internal Medicine

is the medical specialty dealing with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of internal diseases.Physicians specializing in internal medicine are called internists.
