Orthopedic department

Welcome to Orthopedic department!

Orthopedics is the medical specialty that focuses on the prevention and treatment of joints, spine, bones, muscles, connective tissues and other supportive tissues of our bodies (ie ligaments andcartilage) we are able to walk, work and enjoy life through this complex structure.
The importance of orthopedics:

  • Treatment of injuries and diseases of the musculoskeletal system in the body
  • Treatment of joints, ligaments, bones, tendons, nerves and muscles.
  • This helps many people move, work and be active.

The orthopedic department was established in our hospital. This came because with age, it is linked to the gradual loss of bone and muscle mass, as well as bone problems, and the chances of falling, fractures and inability to carry out daily activities.

"A correct diagnosis is the basis for determining the appropriate treatment plan. We are ready to help you"

The Orthopedic Center at Helwan Hospital focuses on health care and treatment of orthopedic injuries. Our specialized orthopedic surgeons are ready to provide comprehensive care with international therapeutic standards, We aim to reduce unnecessary surgery and achieve the best treatment outcome for all of our patients

Services offered at Orthopedic department here:

Musculoskeletal tumor surgery hand surgery Shoulder and elbow surgery Joint replacement surgery
Pediatric orthopedic surgery Foot and ankle surgery spine surgery Trauma surgery
Surgical sports medicine Functional impairment of joint movement torn ligaments Arthritis treatment
