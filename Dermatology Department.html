Dermatology department

Welcome to Dermatology department!

Helwan University Hospital provides specialized surgical and cosmetic medical care for skin, hair and nail conditions, our certified medical and nursing team is committed to providing the highest quality of healthcare to our patients, using pharmaceuticals, skin care and equipment for all FDA-approved treatments.

Our Services:

We provide the diagnosis and treatment of all skin diseases including:

  • Acne.
  • Atopic eczema.
  • Skin tan(Melasma).
  • Eczema.
  • Bacterial and fungal viral diseases.
  • Hair problems, baldness.
  • Excessive sweating of the hand, foot, armpits.
  • Skin diseases from infants to childhood.
  • Nail problems.
  • Pigmentation.
  • Psoriasis.
  • Sexually transmitted diseases.
  • Skin cancer.

Prevention and skin rejuvenation:

We focus on all preventive measures as well as renovation techniques to ensure total wellness and balance of your skin, hair and nails.

Prevention:

Wrinkles: We use high-quality pharmaceuticals, such as sunscreens and nutrients, to protect your skin from environmental damage and age, and we use the latest laser skin rejuvenation with other procedures that help delay signs of aging.
Hair: Our certified hair specialists can provide individual treatment for your own hair problems.

Skin rejuvenation:

Wrinkles: To rejuvenate the skin, we only use laser therapy, botulinum toxin, fillers and chemical peeling (misotherapy) to feed and tighten the areas you want, all of which are fda-compliant.
Color Change: We use the latest laser techniques to achieve the best possible appearance for you in situations such as wineries, birthmarks and skin color change.

conditions:

Conditions such as spider veins are treated through the use of FDA-compliant laser techniques to maintain the highest standards of the International Joint Commission, or by injecting drugs.
Scars: We help facilitate and improve these annoying scars for you through a laser treatment plan or on-demand chemical peeling.
Hair removal: Painless unwanted hair removal in a more successful way than electrolysis.
