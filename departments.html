Departments

Psychology Department

It is the study of human behaviour and mental processes. Psychologists study humans from a variety of perspectives, seeking knowledge about how we perceive, think, remember, decide, feel, act, create and relate to the physical world around us.

Internal Medicine Department

It is a medical specialty in which physicians apply scientific knowledge to diagnose and treat adults with a broad range of health concerns and diseases. Physicians specialized in internal medicine are called internists or general internists.

Department Of Dentistry

It is a branch of medicine that consists of the study, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases, disorders, and conditions of the mouth, and related structures and tissues, particularly in jaw and facial area.

Pediatrics Department

The pediatrician is usually responsible for providing medical care in all its forms to the child as soon as he is born, through several tasks such as: periodic examination of children, efficient diagnosis of diseases, and determining the correct treatment.

Orthopedics Department

It the branch where we study everything about bones and their functions,Also the diseases that affect them,their symptoms, causes and how to deal with them.Also the role of nutrition, sports and alternative medicine in maintaining bone health.

Otorhinolaryngology Department

It is a surgical subspecialty of medicine dealing with ear,nose ,throat, and related organs of the head and neck. Doctors who specialize in this field are called ENT surgeons, or head and neck surgeons.

Ophthalmology Department

It is a branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders.ophthalmologists are allowed to prescribe medications to treat eye diseases, implement laser therapy, and perform surgery when needed.

Obstetrics and Gynecology Department

is a medical specialty that includes subspecialties in both obstetrics ( pregnancy , childbirth and the postpartum period ) and gynecology (health of the female reproductive system)

Accident and Burn Department

It is responsible about all types of burn cases and accidents Our department has a well trained doctors and great services we have (1)Laser burn surgery services, (2) Restoration surgery services.

Cardiology and Intensive Care Department

A cardiac intensive care unit (CICU) provides the highest level of care for ill patients with serious heart conditions.Cardiology is the study and treatment of disorders of the heart and the blood vessels.

surgery Department

It is a medical or dental specialty that uses operative manual and instrumental techniques on a person to treat a pathological condition such as a disease or injury, to help improve bodily function, appearance, or to repair unwanted ruptured areas.

Neurology Department

is the branch of medicine concerned with the study and treatment of disorders of the nervous system. The nervous system is a complex, sophisticated system that regulates and coordinates body activities.

Dermatology department

Dermatologists are medical doctors who specialize in skin, hair and nails. Dermatologists also handle cosmetic disorders, like hair loss and scars. Your dermatologist will examine you, order lab tests, make a diagnosis and treat your condition with medication or a procedure
