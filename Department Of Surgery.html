General Surgery

Welcome to General Surgery department!

General Surgery is the medical Specialty responsible for diagnosing conditions that may need surgery, and then evaluating patients'condition as to whether their health is tolerable to surgical treatment. this Specialty is not only concerned with performing surgical procedures, but also with patient care and care before, during and after the operation. of course, the study of general surgery begins with studying medicine, then moving on to surgery, and Surgery in a specific field.

General surgery doctors are trained to provide:

  • head and neck surgery.
  • pediatric surgery, surgical critical care, surgical oncology,.
  • making a diagnosis; preoperative, operative and postoperative management of the patient.
  • trauma and burns, transplants and vascular surgery..
