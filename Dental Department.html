What is dentist?

The specialty of dentistry is one of the branches of human medicine, which is concerned with the study of the mouth and face, not just the teeth. The importance of studying human medicine lies in the importance of dentistry itself. Teeth are one of the hardest parts of the human body, and one of their most important functions is to chew food, especially the front teeth. It also has an important role in speech outputs. The specialty of dentistry is a surgical medical field concerned with the diagnosis, prevention and treatment of diseases or injuries in the teeth, especially the jaws and oral cavity. Dentistry has become one of the top fields of study of choice for many students, especially after the tremendous technological progress in radiology and the materials used in the treatment of diseases, restorations and cosmetic dentistry. And dentistry has an ancient history dating back to seven thousand BC. Therefore, the cultural and technological development has created a boom in this specialty, which is considered today one of the most important medical specialties.

Brief introduction to dentistry

It is a medical specialty concerned with the teeth in terms of their strength, treatment of diseases, especially in the jaws, beautification, protection and preservation of a beautiful appearance. Therefore, the specialty of dentistry is divided into several different sections. Including the department of cosmetic dentistry, the department of whitening and restoration, the department of fillings and many other departments.

Dental Care

Brief Summary :

There are certain criteria for choosing a toothbrush and toothpaste according to health status and age. Neglecting to clean the teeth leads to the accumulation of a layer of plaque on the surfaces of the teeth. Teeth should be cleaned twice a day for two minutes each time. You must visit the dentist periodically every six months. Most children enjoy using an electric toothbrush,which can simulate their interest in their teeth.

Introduction :

Cleaning the teeth with a toothbrush and toothpaste correctly and daily is the most important way to maintain oral and dental hygiene and prevent cavities and the resulting diseases.Also,taking care of your toothbrush is an important component of a clean and healthy mouth.Dental health of the childe ,as well as its prevention,and and plays an important role in improving health,as most children between the ages of five to nine years suffer from tooth decay,which is the main reason that leads to hospitalization and school absenteeism.

The importance of cleaning teeth

Preventing the accumulation of plaque on the teeth (a sticky and thin layer formed on the surface of the teeth from food remnants and containing bacteria that cause gum disease and tooth decay). Prevention of gingivitis. Save time and money for dental problems. Get fresh breath,by preventing the formation of odor-causing bacteria. Reducing the formation of external pigmentation on the surfaces of the teeth

Services provided

General dental services :

The Corporation provides general dental services in all its health centers, which are provided by qualified and well-trained general dental practitioners for cases that fall within the scope of their health practice, in accordance with the guidelines of the Qatar Council for Health Specialties and within the scope of primary health care:

  • Clinical and radiological examination.
  • Dental diagnosis.
  • Simple tooth extraction.
  • Dental restorations.
  • Periodontal disease treatment.
  • Anterior tooth root canal treatment and urgent posterior tooth treatment.
  • Children's dental care

Specialized Dental Services :

Specialized dental care is often provided as part of a patient's treatment plan and is available at designated health care centers for all corporate clients. This service is provided by well-trained and qualified dental professionals to deal with cases that are beyond the scope of service of general dental practitioners, but remain within the scope of primary health care:

  • mouth surgery.
  • Advanced treatment of gum disease.
  • Advanced treatment of dental nerves.
  • Advanced treatment of dental nerves.
  • Dental laser treatment.
