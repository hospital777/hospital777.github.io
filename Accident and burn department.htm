Accident and burn department

Welcome to accident and burn department!

Accident and burn department at Helwan University Hospital is considered as first class services build upon the international standards and latest medical guidelines,physicians and nurses who are on a high degree of specialization and acceleration in performance and experience in quick dealing with cirtical accident & burn situations 24 hours per day and provide immediate care for the all diseases.

All department staff are classified and are subject to regular training and continuous education. The department features many Laser burn surgery services and Restoration services, including :

Restoration surgery services:


  • Facial repair surgery.
  • Skull deformities surgery.
  • Facial and maxillofam surgery.
  • Finger repair surgery.
  • Hand and foot surgery.

Laser burn surgery services:


  • Fractional laser surgery.
  • Carbon dioxide and perbiam lasers surgery.
  • Carbon laser surgery.
  • Using lasers without removing layers surgery.

Tips before laser burn surgery:


  1. Stop smoking two weeks before the operation.
  2. Stop liquidity medications such as aspirin.
  3. Do not use cosmetics containing certain substances such as glycolic acid and retinol one or two weeks before the operation.
  4. Do not be exposed to sunlight for long periods while wearing sunglasses.
  5. Tell your doctor if you're at risk of lip truncups, which are red pills that occur with cold roles, so your doctor can prescribe appropriate treatment to prevent this from happening.
